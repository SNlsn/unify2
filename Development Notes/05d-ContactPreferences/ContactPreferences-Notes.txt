Contact_Preferences (join table)

establishes the collection of items representing menu of post production preferences for a Contact

Database attributes:
  contact_id      integer (use "company_id:integer:index" refer to
                          http://railscasts.com/episodes/318-upgrading-to-rails-3-2?view=asciicast)
  preference_id   integer (use "company_id:integer:index" refer to
                          http://railscasts.com/episodes/318-upgrading-to-rails-3-2?view=asciicast)
